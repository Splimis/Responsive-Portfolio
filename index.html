About Me


I was born in St. Louis, Missouri and almost immediately moved to Lawrence, New York. Some time later I moved to Freehold, New Jersey and graduated from Freehold Township Highschool. Upon graduation I attended college at Wagner in Staten Island, New York. After my third symester I dropped out, as there is no money in being a music major. I then attended Lincoln Technical Institue in Newark and graduated with a 3.87 avarage. I have been working in the automotive field ever since.

In the mean time, I enjoy hiking, writing music, and playing in local bands.
